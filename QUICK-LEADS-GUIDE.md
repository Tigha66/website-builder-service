# 🎯 FIND 50 LEADS IN 60 MINUTES - ULTRA-FAST GUIDE

## **THE 1-HOUR LEAD GENERATION SYSTEM**

---

## ⏱️ **MINUTE 0-5: Setup**

1. Open Google Sheets or Excel
2. Create these columns:
   - Business Name
   - Niche
   - Phone
   - Email (if found)
   - Address
   - City
   - Website (Y/N)
   - Notes
   - Status

3. Open in one tab: **Google Maps** (maps.google.com)

---

## ⏱️ **MINUTE 5-35: Google Maps Method** (30 minutes = 30 leads)

**Process (repeat 30 times, 1 minute per lead):**

1. **Search:** "plumber manchester" (or any city + niche)
2. **Scroll** through the map results
3. **Click** on each business that has:
   - 4+ star rating
   - 10+ reviews
   - Looks established
4. **Check** the "Website" field
5. **If NO website** or looks outdated → **ADD TO LIST**
6. **Copy** their:
   - Name
   - Phone (always listed)
   - Address
   - City
   - From their Google listing

**Pro Tip:** Many businesses have phone but no email - that's OK! You can call them.

**Target:** 30 leads in 30 minutes (1 minute each)

---

## ⏱️ **MINUTE 35-50: Yell.com Method** (15 minutes = 15 leads)

1. Go to **Yell.com**
2. Search "electrician birmingham" (or any niche + city)
3. Scroll through results
4. Click businesses WITHOUT website badge
5. Copy their details
6. Add to your list

**Target:** 15 more leads

---

## ⏱️ **MINUTE 50-60: Facebook Method** (10 minutes = 5-10 leads)

1. Go to **Facebook.com**
2. Search "plumber near me" or "[city] plumber"
3. Look for businesses with:
   - Active Facebook page
   - Good reviews
   - NO website link in About section
4. Add to list

**Target:** 5-10 more leads

---

## 🎯 **TOTAL AFTER 60 MINUTES:**

- **30 leads** from Google Maps
- **15 leads** from Yell.com
- **5-10 leads** from Facebook
- **= 50-55 REAL LEADS** ✅
