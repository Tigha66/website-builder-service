## 📋 **LEAD RESEARCH PROCESS**

### **Where to Find Leads:**

**UK Sources:**
1. **Yell.com** - UK business directory
2. **ThomsonLocal.com** - Local business listings
3. **Checkatrade.com** - Trade professionals
4. **Google Maps** - Search "[niche] near me [city]"
5. **Facebook Business Pages** - Many have only FB, no website
6. **Instagram** - Service businesses with only social presence
7. **TrustATrader.com** - Verified tradespeople
8. **Bark.com** - Local service requests

**US Sources:**
1. **YellowPages.com** - US business directory
2. **Yelp.com** - Many businesses have only Yelp, no website
3. **Angi.com** (formerly Angie's List) - Home services
4. **Thumbtack.com** - Local professionals
5. **Google Maps** - Search "[niche] near me [city]"
6. **Facebook Business Pages**
7. **Nextdoor.com** - Local recommendations
8. **Better Business Bureau** - BBB accredited businesses

### **Verification Checklist:**

For each business, check:
- [ ] Business is real and active (call to verify if needed)
- [ ] No original website OR website is outdated/broken
- [ ] Has some online presence (FB, Google listing, directory)
- [ ] Good reviews/reputation (4+ stars)
- [ ] Owner contact info available
- [ ] Appears to be making decent revenue (can afford website)

### **Red Flags (Skip These):**
- ❌ Business closed or inactive
- ❌ Already has modern, professional website
- ❌ Bad reviews (under 3.5 stars)
- ❌ No contact info available
- ❌ Appears to be a chain/franchise (decision makers elsewhere)
